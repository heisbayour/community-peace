About C.O.P.E.R

The Community Peace Reporters Network (C.O.P.E.R) is committed to building peaceful communities through ethical reporting, mediation, and citizen engagement.

Our Mission & Vision

We aim to empower communities with accurate reporting, conflict resolution strategies, and collaborative peace-building initiatives.

Our Mission

To document, report, and resolve community conflicts responsibly while promoting understanding and collaboration.

Our Vision

To create communities where peace, transparency, and mutual respect thrive, and conflicts are addressed constructively.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, inclusiveness, empathy, and a commitment to peaceful resolution guide every action we take.
