Building Peaceful Communities Together

The Community Peace Reporters Network (C.O.P.E.R) is dedicated to documenting, reporting, and resolving community conflicts through peaceful journalism and citizen engagement.

What We Do

Our mission is to promote peace and understanding in communities through responsible reporting and conflict resolution.

Peace Reporting

Training community members to report on conflicts with a focus on peace-building and solutions journalism.

Mediation

Facilitating dialogue between conflicting parties to find common ground and peaceful resolutions.

Community Building

Organizing events and workshops that bring community members together to foster understanding.
